Televisions and Energy Use (Australia)

Televisions use electricity mainly based on screen size, brightness settings, and the display technology (e.g., LED vs OLED). In the Australian market, checking energy labels and adjusting settings can help reduce power use.

Televisions Currently Available in Australia

  • High brightness can increase energy consumption.
  • Standby power can add up if devices are left plugged in.
